THE WOLF OF WALL STREET

Justin Costa

April 21, 2026

The Wolf of Wall Street is funny, goofy, fast-paced, and entertaining. The Film is based on a true story. Leonardo Dicaprio performance as Jordan Belfort is great. This movie feels like chaos. Overall, its a memorable film.
