Shutter Island

Justin Costa

April 21, 2026

Review

Shutter Island is a psychological thriller that keeps you thinking from start to finish. The setting is set in 1954, two detectives are going to an island to investigate the disappearance of a patient in a mental institution. Leonardo DiCaprio delivers a performance that is peak and the plot twists make the movie intense and unpredictable.

Overall, the film is dark, emotional, and very well made. It’s a great choice if you enjoy mystery and suspense. Peak.

Shutter Island Trivia

  • The movie is based on a Novel by Dennis Dehane.
  • Filmed partly in Massachusetts
  • The Setting is set in 1954
